About Us....

Nature World was was created in 2020, with its small yet passionate team, Its main goal is to nuture an interest and educate the young people of today in the importance of looking after the world around us. Through our website and our quartely magazine, the natural world and all its marvels is always at your finger tips. Nature world through its vast affilations with other world recognised conservation groups aims to give the youth of today what we all have previously inherited.
