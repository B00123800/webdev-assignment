South America

About South America:

* South America is a huge continent with a wide range of climates and terrains. You have everything from the snow covered Andes Mountains as one extreme to the dry La Guajira Desert located in Colombia

* With over three thousand species of birds South America is often referred to as the bird continent.

* Toucans have larger bills, in comparison to the size of their body, than any other bird in the world it helps them keep cool in the hot environment in which they live.

* Many animals found on this continent are not found anywhere else in the world.
